The Fitness App Fit For The Modern Parent

Welcome to FamBod! Our goal is to help busy and tired moms and dads stay committed to their fitness and health. By adding fun and short workouts into the quality time you spend with your little ones, you can keep your Mom and Dad Bods fit and healthy.

How Does It Work?

A Fitness Plan, Just For You

When you sign up for FamBod, we will ask you for some basic information about you and your child. We will use this to create a personalized fitness plan for you that will evolve with you as you advance on your fitness journey.

Exercise Without Skipping a Beat

Our exercises are designed to be completed while you spend time with your child. Whether you’re in playtime or bedtime, FamBod can help you to add a few minutes of exercise, without missing a single moment with your child.

Stay on Track

FamBod’s fitness plan works by offering a variety of exercises that you can complete whenever you can squeeze them into your schedule. We will make sure to remind you to fit a quick workout in, and recommend exercises for your quickly complete.

Progress, Step by Step

By completing the weekly exercises, your fitness level will improve. FamBod will help you track your progress and automatically evolve your fitness plan to keep up with you.
